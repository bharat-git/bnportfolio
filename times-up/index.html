Time's up

Objective

A mobile application to trade time instead of money in return for pretty much anything like buying groceries from a supermarket or getting your car fixed from a garage.

User Persona (Proto-Persona)

Scenario map

This a specific and one of the most important scenario map of a user hiring a service provider. The blue notes are the actions that a potential user will perform to hire a service provider. The yellow notes are the ideas of how the application will assist a user in performing the next action.

 

Wireframes

First Iteration ...

After a few rounds of user review from my friends and colleagues following are the key points that I felt should be included in the application.

1) Indication of remaining hours throughout the application so that the user can keep a check and hire service providers accordingly.

2)Addition of ‘Home’ and a ‘side-nav’ button (included in the top of every screen) so that users can navigate through screens seamlessly.

3) Field to apply offers before transacting and buttons to cancel and schedule a service.
